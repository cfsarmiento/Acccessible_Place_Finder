About

Our Mission

We wanted to create an application so people with various accessibility needs could plan ahead and know the levels of access of a place before arrival. Users can now view a map of locations with the highest accessibility ratings and be confident that a business they are planning on visiting has what they need.

Access Needs

Ramps

Have you ever shown up to someplace and realized there was no way to make it through the front door? This specification makes sure a location has at least one ramped access way in for people unable to make it up stairs or unlevel entrance ways.
